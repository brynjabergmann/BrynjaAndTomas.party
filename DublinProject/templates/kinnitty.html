Kinnitty Castle Hotel

To book a room, you can either contact the hotel directly by ringing +353 (0)57 9137318 or emailing reception@kinnittycastlehotel.com. Make sure to mention that you are a guest at the wedding of Brynja Bergmann and Tómas Helgason.
There are four kinds of rooms in Kinnitty Castle Hotel: Standard Rooms, Large Standard Rooms, Large Rooms and Superior Rooms. More details can be found by clicking on the table below. Notice that the prices are for per person and are based on two people sharing. In the event of a 3rd person they will be charged the per person rate. Similarly, if it is a single room, there will be a supplement of €50 added to the per person rate. The rooms do either have a double bed or a twin bed, so make sure you ask for the one you prefer.
