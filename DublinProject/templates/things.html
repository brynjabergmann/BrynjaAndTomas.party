Things to do in Dublin

Dublin is the capital of Ireland, with a population of just over a million. The River Liffey splits Dublin into two areas, the Northside and the Southside. Irish (Gaelic) is spoken as a first language in some small areas in Ireland, but as a second language by a larger group. That is why most signs are bilingual - in both English and Irish. Irish people are in general very friendly and polite - so much that they thank the bus driver when they get off the bus! Dublin has everything; history, culture, music, shops, good food and of course, beer! In the table below we have listed a few places and activities that we recommend for people staying in Dublin.
